Want to learn meteor? Here are some good guides.

  • www.discovermeteor.com

    Discover Meteor by Sacha Greif & Tom Coleman takes you from a beginer to journeyman meteor levels.

  • docs.meteor.com

    The official Meteor docs are pretty good too. If you know javascript you can get your app out in a few hours.

  • youtube.com/user/MeteorVideos

    If you like videos, you should watch the meteor youtube channel. Has screencasts and video lectures.

  • meteorhacks.com/meteor-weekly

    You can also subscribe to the meteor weekly. Weekly awesomeness, right in your box.
